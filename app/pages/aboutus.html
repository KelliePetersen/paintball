About Lowell Paintball

Professionally Run | Incredibly Fun | Welcome to Anyone

We first opened back in YEAR, and ever since then, we've focused on being the best place to play Paintball not only in Carrollton, but the entire West Georgia area. Our focus is to have a facility that is professionally run, incredibly fun, and welcome to anyone. We cater to people of all ages and skill levels, and do our best to ensure that our guests have a safe and enjoyable time.

With our caterer we can provide a fast, fun company picnic that is also beneficial to business. Return to work on Monday a stronger team! Perfect for youth groups, clubs, groups of friends, families, or any other gathering where people want to have fun.

If you'd like to learn more about us, feel free to call us at 770-854-CAMP. If you can't get through, try 404-291-6190. You can also email us at atch@campblood.com.
