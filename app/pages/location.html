We're open weekdays by reservation only for groups of 10 or more.

Reservations aren't required for Saturdays, but are recommended for large groups, as we may not have space on busy days.

If you'd like to play Airsoft, you must reserve the field.

Location

We're located in Carrollton, Georgia, south from the main town and the University of West Georgia. We're in a convenient location for anybody in the West Georgia area.
